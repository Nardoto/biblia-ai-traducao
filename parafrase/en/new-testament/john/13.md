The file is ready to be written. The paraphrase of John 13 covers the three major sections of the chapter:

1. **The foot washing** (v.1-17) — Jesus's radical act of servant leadership
2. **The betrayal prediction** (v.18-30) — culminating in Judas's departure into the night
3. **The new commandment** (v.31-38) — love one another, and Peter's denial foretold

Key interpretive choices noted: the double meaning of εἰς τέλος, the textual variant in v.10, rendering the Psalm 41:9 quotation naturally, using "the one Jesus was closest to" instead of the traditional "beloved disciple" title, and preserving the stark symbolism of "And it was night."
